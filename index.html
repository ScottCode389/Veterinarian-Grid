About Us

Northern Kentucky Pet Care Clinic is a full service veterinary clinic that will meet all of your pet's veterinary needs. Our staff will provide you and your pet with professionally designed pet wellness programs as well as medical treatment for pets that have suffered an illness or injury. We welcome "pocket pets" such as rabbits and hamsters, birds, and of course dogs and cats. Our staff is prepared to offer emergency vet services any time that our office is open, and we partner with an emergency after-hours clinic that is close by.

Our Office

Our veterinary clinic is dedicated to the care and wellness of pets in Northern Kentucky. Our office is clean and comfortable and we offer a beautiful fenced-in outdoor area behind the office where you can take your pet if they have anxiety about being in an office or if they need a potty break!


We also offer cozy facilities for surgical patients who need to stay with us during recovery. A technician remains with any of our patients that requires overnight care.
