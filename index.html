About Us

Northern Kentucky Pet Care Clinic is a full service veterinary clinic that will meet all of your pet veterinary needs, including pet chiropractic care and acupuncture. We also offer puppy classes and behavior consults to help new puppy owners. Our staff of two veterinarians and four assistants provide you and your pet with professionally designed pet wellness programs that include vaccinations and parasite prevention. We also provide medical treatment for pets that have suffered an illness or injury. We also welcome exotic animals, "pocket pets" such as rabbits and ferrets, birds, and of course dogs and cats. Our staff is prepared to offer emergency vet services any time that our office is open, and we partner with an emergency after-hours clinic that is just 5 miles away.

Our Office

Our veterinary clinic is dedicated to the care and wellness of pets in Northern Kentucky. Our office is clean and comfortable and we offer a beautiful fenced-in outdoor area behind the office where you can take your pet if they have anxiety about being in an office or if they need a potty break!
